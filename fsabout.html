Meet the Team

The YumTime team is a part of an organization called Girls Who Code Adobe. GWC Adobe is trying to teach young girls about code. As a final project we all banded together with righteousness in our hearts to make a website to help those in need. We all knew that we wanted to do a project about food because who doesn't love food? We actually were at first deciding whether we wanted this to just be a game with a message about food or a website. Obviously we opted for a website because we thought it'd be able to make more of an impact and be more useful.
